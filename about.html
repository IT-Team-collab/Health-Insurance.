Frequently Asked Questions

What makes HealthCare Plus different from other insurers?

HealthCare Plus stands out through our member-centered approach, digital innovation, extensive healthcare provider network, and commitment to preventive care. We focus on making healthcare accessible and affordable while providing exceptional customer service.

How quickly can I expect claims to be processed?

Most claims are processed within 7-10 business days. With our digital claims system, you can track the status of your claim in real-time through our mobile app or online portal, making the process transparent and stress-free.

Does HealthCare Plus cover pre-existing conditions?

Yes, we cover pre-existing conditions after a waiting period of 12 months for most conditions. Some plans offer shorter waiting periods, and we provide specialized programs to help manage chronic conditions during this time.

Can I add family members to my plan after enrollment?

Absolutely! You can add dependents to your plan at any time. New additions such as newborns can be added within 30 days of birth without waiting periods. For other family members, standard enrollment guidelines apply.

What digital tools do you offer to members?

Our digital toolkit includes a user-friendly mobile app and web portal where you can view your benefits, find providers, submit and track claims, access telehealth services, store medical records securely, and communicate with our customer care team.

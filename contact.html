Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a health plan?

You can enroll through our online portal by visiting the Enrollment page, or contact our enrollment specialists at +256-7718-30-421 for personalized assistance. The enrollment process typically takes 15-20 minutes to complete.

How long does claim processing take?

Most claims are processed within 7-10 business days after submission. You can check the status of your claim anytime through our online portal or by contacting our claims department directly at claims@healthcareplus.com.

Can I change my plan during the year?

Yes, you can change your plan during the annual enrollment period which typically runs from November 1 to December 15. You may also qualify for a Special Enrollment Period if you experience certain life events like marriage, having a baby, or losing other health coverage.

What documents do I need for enrollment?

You'll need identification (National ID or passport), proof of residence, and relevant medical history documents. For family coverage, you'll also need information for each family member, including their identification and any pre-existing medical conditions.

How can I find a healthcare provider in my network?

You can find in-network providers through our online provider directory on our website. Simply log in to your account, navigate to the "Find a Provider" section, and search by location, specialty, or provider name. You can also call our customer service for assistance.

What should I do if I have a medical emergency?

In case of a medical emergency, seek medical attention immediately. You or someone on your behalf should notify us within 48 hours of the emergency treatment. All emergency care is covered according to your plan benefits, regardless of whether the facility is in-network.
